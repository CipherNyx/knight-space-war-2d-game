# 🛡️ Knight Space War – 2D Game

A **2D space adventure game** built with **JavaScript, HTML, and CSS**, where a brave knight battles alien missiles in outer space.  
Developed in **2021** as part of my **Software Engineering degree assignment** at *Java Institute for Advanced Technology*.

---

## 🎮 Game Overview
- 🚀 Play as a knight in space fighting against alien missiles  
- 🪙 Collect coins and destroy missiles to gain XP  
- ⚔️ Use melee attacks to survive and progress  
- 🎵 Toggle music and fullscreen mode in settings  
- 🏆 Reach **10,000 XP** to complete Level 1 and win the game

## 🕹️ Controls
- **Enter** → Melee Attack  
- **Arrow Keys** → Move Knight  
- **Settings Menu** → Toggle Music & Fullscreen
